| Signal | Strength | Count Needed |
|--------|----------|--------------|

- [ ] 3+ companies say "yes we'd pay $X for this"
- [ ] 5+ companies request samples
- [ ] 1+ company offers to pre-pay or LOI
- [ ] Clear pattern on which dataset has most demand
